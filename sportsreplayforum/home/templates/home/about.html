About

Our aim is to help sports fans determine whether it is worth spending their time watching an event.

No one likes to spend 2hrs watching a dull race or a 0-0 match when there are so many other things to be doing.

You can check our rating system to see how other fans and our continuously improving AI rating system will recommend to watch.

And you can help other users by registering and rating events yourself.


Due to rights issues and restrictions, we are unable to provide replay streams. Use your favourite sports package or online stream to find replays

Roadmap

Here are some additional features that we plan to add in the future:

  • Further integration of AI ratings, so that users don't have to wait until a user has provided a rating, they can get an idea of how the event was straight away.
  • Better integration of highlights from various sources
  • Actual results embedded into the Spoiler Zone
  • Sport news feed embedded into the Spoiler Zone
  • Forum for users to comment on events
  • Like button on ratings so users can agree/disagree with ratings
